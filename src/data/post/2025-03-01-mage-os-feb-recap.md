## ****Content and Community Initiatives**:**

MageOS is evolving into a **tech incubator**, focusing on innovation and making development easier for the community. This shift aims to encourage research, improve tools, and create a more efficient ecosystem for Magento-based solutions. The goal is to support developers, merchants, and businesses of all sizes by making the platform more accessible and user-friendly.

In other news, an upcoming article featuring **[David Lambauer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/david-lambauer/)** will dive into **[Dev Docs v1.0](https://devdocs.mage-os.org/docs/main)**. This article will take readers behind the scenes, exploring how the documentation was developed, the challenges faced during its creation, and the future improvements planned to enhance the developer experience. Keep an eye out for this update as we continue to enhance the platform’s documentation and resources!

### **Community & Platform Updates**

###### **[MageOS 1.0.6 & Mirror 2.4.7-p4 Released](https://mage-os.org/releases/release-mage-os-distribution-1-0-6/)**

MageOS 1.0.6 and the latest **Magento Mirror 2.4.7-p4** are now available! Developers should note that **Adobe Payment Services** will need updates in the next release cycle.

###### **Mage2TV Videos & Cache Cleaner Tool**

Exciting news—**Vinai** is making all **Mage2TV** videos available on **YouTube** for free! Additionally, there’s talk about moving the **Cache Cleaner tool** under the MageOS namespace since so many people use it.

#### Development Progress

**GrapesJS Page Builder Integration ([#57](https://tree.taiga.io/project/mage-os-mage-os-technical-initiatives/us/57?kanban-status=9925172))**

Discussions are underway to introduce **[GrapesJS](https://discordapp.com/channels/893449664093904936/950670671145467924/1342045660337143871)**, a JavaScript-based page builder, as a more flexible and user-friendly way to manage CMS content. [Davide Lunardon](https://www.linkedin.com/in/davide-lunardon-b78813a1?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAABWn28wBoeCdysd3NwSi8RMpNTWJxKTofjg&lipi=urn%3Ali%3Apage%3Ad_flagship3_search_srp_all%3B1lQuBolEQ3aw%2B0GKklcQvA%3D%3D) proposed this integration to simplify the development process while keeping Magento’s existing Page Builder intact. The main challenge is balancing ease of use with maintaining consistent design standards. Further discussions and testing will follow.

###### **Reservations Grid Improvements ([#34](https://tree.taiga.io/project/mage-os-mage-os-technical-initiatives/us/34?kanban-status=9349607))**

[David Tabatadze](https://www.linkedin.com/in/david-tabatadze-981055213?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAADXvLDEBL1hn1kNQbViFWt7fL5pS1_wxLL4&lipi=urn%3Ali%3Apage%3Ad_flagship3_search_srp_all%3B%2FladTy3aQ8K1stDA5YZDPw%3D%3D) has made great progress on **Reservations Grid enhancements**, adding an easy-to-use UI for managing reservations. The PR is complete and is awaiting final reviews and testing before being merged.

###### **Terraform to OpenTofu Migration ([#37](https://tree.taiga.io/project/mage-os-mage-os-technical-initiatives/us/37?kanban-status=9349607))**

The migration from **Terraform to OpenTofu** is officially complete. A final review is required before merging the changes, ensuring everything works as expected.

###### **AI Translation Module Updates (#32)**

[Davide Lunardon](https://www.linkedin.com/in/davide-lunardon-b78813a1?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAABWn28wBoeCdysd3NwSi8RMpNTWJxKTofjg&lipi=urn%3Ali%3Apage%3Ad_flagship3_search_srp_all%3B1lQuBolEQ3aw%2B0GKklcQvA%3D%3D) and [Sam](https://www.linkedin.com/in/samuele-martini-02820795?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAABQkV2ABtzxE7szV3KAOMFiWtoqGeYXs0rc&lipi=urn%3Ali%3Apage%3Ad_flagship3_search_srp_all%3BR5Kzi%2F0iRSuXNYPmmxK3gA%3D%3D) made updates to the **AI-powered translation module**, resolving key issues with **configurable product translations**. A few smaller fixes are still in the pipeline.

###### **Backend AI Assistant ([#19](https://tree.taiga.io/project/mage-os-mage-os-technical-initiatives/us/19?kanban-status=9349606))**

A **Backend AI Assistant** module is ready for Mage-OS! The next step is to set up a repository in Mage-OS Lab so it can be shared and further developed by the community.
