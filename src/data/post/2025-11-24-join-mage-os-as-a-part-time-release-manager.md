**Release Manager**

**About Mage-OS**

Mage-OS exists to Accelerate Change & Pioneer Innovation in the Magento Open Source ecosystem. We are an open-source community project building a truly open, modern, and sustainable eCommerce platform based on a fork of Magento 2. Our mission is to ensure the longevity, accessibility, and innovation of the Magento ecosystem. 

We’re looking for a **Release Manager** to help coordinate, stabilize, and improve our release processes. This is a great fit for someone who understands Magento deeply, enjoys structured releases, and loves helping an open-source project thrive.**Contract Position Summary:**

Mage-OS is seeking an experienced and trustworthy Release Manager to join our team. The Release Manager will oversee the end-to-end release process, ensuring timely and quality delivery of software updates and new features. This role involves coordination with various team members, managing release schedules, and maintaining the integrity of the release pipeline. Reporting to the Mage-OS technical lead.

**Key Responsibilities:**

#### **Leadership & Planning**

- **Manage release schedules**, ensuring alignment with project goals and community priorities.  
    

- **Coordinate with staff and contributors** to deliver smooth, reliable releases.

**Technical Oversight & Execution**

- **Manage version control and branching strategies** (Git/GitHub) to maintain codebase stability.  
    

- **Conduct code reviews and provide feedback** to ensure quality, consistency, and adherence to Magento standards.  
    

- **Enforce** release criteria and maintain high technical standards.  
    

- **Maintain and improve release pipelines** (CI/CD), continuously improving efficiency and reliability.  
    

#### **Documentation & Communication**

- **Prepare and publish release notes**, summarizing key changes for both developers and users.  
    

- **Maintain and improve release documentation** to ensure transparency and repeatability.  
    

- **Attend weekly technical meetings** (Tuesdays, 3 PM CET on Discord) to stay aligned with the community and technical team.

**Requirements:**

- Strong knowledge of Magento 2 (both front-end and back-end development).

- Familiarity with Magento coding standards and best practices.

- Understanding of web performance optimization techniques.

- Proficiency with CI/CD pipelines and version control systems, particularly Git and GitHub.

- Proficiency in PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and MySQL.

- A couple of references known in the global Magento community that we can speak to.  
    

**What We Offer**

- A meaningful role in a high-impact open-source project.

- Flexible working hours — about ~8 hours per week.

- €1,000/month compensation.

- The chance to work with a passionate, globally recognized Magento community.
