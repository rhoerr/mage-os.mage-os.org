Due to the funds raised from our generous partners and members since we opened membership last September, we're now looking to make our first hire to accelerate our growth even further!

The initial role we're looking to fill is for a part-time Project Manager / Chief of Staff, and you can see the job description below.

To apply or for more information please email [board@mage-os.org](mailto:board@mage-os.org) by Friday, March 22.

* * *

### Job Description

##### Role

Project Manager / Chief of Staff (part time)

##### Why we need you

- As we grow, we have lots of volunteers among the 1000 members on Discord, with many projects, ideas, and desire to contribute

- We want to move faster. As people all have day jobs, your mission is to make it easier for more people to contribute (break down tasks, follow up, etc.)

##### Core Tasks

- Attend all the Mage-OS meetings:
    - Content Mondays 4pm CET
    
    - Tech Tuesdays 3pm CET
    
    - Board Wednesdays 3pm CET

- Drive the agenda, and determine tasks, owners, next steps, and timelines

- Follow up on tasks and commitments to see things through

- Onboard new people, show them around, and find a place for them

- Help with governance (General Assemblies) with the Secretary

##### Added Responsibilities based on availability

- Project manage legal work relating to the association

- Help manage the budget for each activity with the Treasurer

- Organize events presence (online and physical) to engage and nurture the community

- Explore and pursue potential opportunities for partnership

##### Must have

- Understanding of the Magento community and ecosystem

- Doer, a sense of responsibility, drive for action

- Fluent in English

- Work in EU and NA timezones

- Comfortable managing multiple stakeholders

- Influential skills (to encourage contributors who are volunteers)

##### Nice to have

- A good reputation in the Magento community

- Extroverted tendencies

- Polish language is a plus

- All backgrounds and identities welcome to apply

##### What you get

- Salary range: up to €12,000 per year based on 8-16 hours per week  
    Negotiable based on experience and available hours

- Remote work, Flexibility (except for the Committee times)

- Leading involvement at the forefront of the Magento ecosystem

- A strong sense of purpose and belonging

- Great organizational culture and work environment where you will feel rewarded, appreciated, and motivated

* * *

### Apply now

Email us your CV and why you're interested to [board@mage-os.org](mailto:board@mage-os.org) by March 22.
