The **Mage-OS Association** is a non-profit organization formed by the Magento community to represent and further the interests of merchants, developers, agencies, and all stakeholders in the ecosystem.

## Our Mission

**We exist to grow the ecosystem by accelerating change and pioneering innovation in the Magento platform.**

### Our Goals

- **Grow the community** and increase market share
- **Nurture positive values** and collaborative culture
- **Sustain Magento** as a leading custom ecommerce platform
- **Increase confidence** in the ecosystem's long-term viability

---

## Our Values

### Community First
Every decision prioritizes ecosystem health over individual interests. We are 100% community-owned and operated—no shareholders, no investors.

### Transparency
Open roadmaps, public discussions, and democratic governance ensure accountability and trust.

### Innovation
We embrace change, experimentation, and continuous improvement to keep the platform competitive.

### Inclusivity
All voices matter. From individual developers to enterprise agencies, everyone has a seat at the table.

---

## How We Accomplish Our Mission

- **Coordinate and fund development** of Mage-OS Distribution
- **Create quality content** supporting platform adoption
- **Fund and support events** aligning with our goals
- **Provide documentation and certification** paths
- **Support organizations** empowering the ecosystem
- **Provide leads and job opportunities** for members
- **Deliver insights and research** to guide strategy

---

## Our Story

Mage-OS began with an **Open Letter about the future of Magento**, signed by **1,641 community members** who depend on the platform for their livelihoods. That letter sparked global discussions and collective action.

From those conversations, Mage-OS was born—dedicated to building:

1. An **independent distribution** enhancing Magento Open Source
2. A **sustainable organization** supporting long-term ecosystem growth
