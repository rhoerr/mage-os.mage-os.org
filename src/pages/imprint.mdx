## Website Operator

The website **mage-os.org** is operated by the Mage-OS Association, a registered non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and advancing the Magento open-source ecosystem through community-driven development, education, and coordination.

### Purpose

The Mage-OS Association was founded to enable the long-term success of the Magento ecosystem by empowering the community to maintain and develop the Mage-OS platform through communication, coordination, and funding. The Association operates in accordance with its [Statutes](/about/statutes).

## Governance

### Board of Directors

The Mage-OS Association is governed by an elected Board of Directors. Current members and their roles can be found on the [Leadership page](/about/leadership).

The Board includes the following officer positions:

- President
- Vice President
- Treasurer
- Actuary

### Business Control Committee

An independent Business Control Committee provides oversight and ensures accountability and transparency in all Association operations. Details are available on the [Leadership page](/about/leadership#business-control-committee).
